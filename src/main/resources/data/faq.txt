Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)
=================================

Q: What is your return policy?
A: We offer a 30-day no-questions-asked return policy. Items must be unused and in original packaging. Contact support@example.com to initiate a return.

Q: How long does shipping take?
A: Standard shipping takes 3-5 business days. Express shipping (1-2 days) is available for an additional fee of ¥25.

Q: Do you ship internationally?
A: Currently we ship to mainland China, Hong Kong, and Taiwan. International shipping for other regions is coming soon.

Q: How can I track my order?
A: After placing an order, you will receive a tracking number by email within 24 hours. You can track your package on the carrier's website.

Q: What payment methods are accepted?
A: We accept WeChat Pay, Alipay, UnionPay, and major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard).

Q: How do I contact customer support?
A: Email: support@example.com | Phone: 400-888-0000 (Mon-Fri 9:00-18:00) | WeChat: ExampleSupport

Q: Is there a warranty on products?
A: All electronics come with a 1-year manufacturer warranty. Furniture items have a 6-month warranty. Warranty does not cover physical damage.

Q: Can I change or cancel my order?
A: Orders can be modified or cancelled within 2 hours of placement. After that, the order is processed and cannot be changed.
